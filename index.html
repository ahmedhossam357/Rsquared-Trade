About US

A diversified family business.
THRIVING SINCE 1981.

RSQUARED HOLDING began its journey in 1981 by the guidance of CEO and Founder Eng.Abdel Raouf Hussein by establishing RSQUARED CONSTRUCTION. RSQUARED CONSTRUCTION focused on all kinds of infrastructure projects through out Egypt. After a while RSQUARED CONSTRUCTION concentrated all of its work force on Road construction projects in Egypt, Sudan and Iraq.

After the outstanding success and the increasing profits year after year at RSQUARED CONSTRUCTION, Eng. Abdel Raouf Hussein saw growth potential in the Real Estate sector. In 1990 Skylight for Tourism and Development was established. Skylight bought 500,000 square metres of land by the Red Sea in Ain el Sokhna, which now holds El Wadi Resort and Horizon hotel.

In 2000 RSQUARED DEVELOPMENT was establish to cater the real estate and housing sector. RSQUARED DEVELOPMENT holds a variety of projects through out Egypt, including apartments, duplexes, villas, and office buildings.

In 2010 Eng. Abdel Raouf saw growth potential in the Agricultural sector in Sudan. Egyment For Agricultural Development was established on a 4000 acres of land in Khartoum, Sudan to produce clover seeds, Mango and Strawberries.

After the Financial Crisis the US market was down substantially. RSQUARED INVESTMENTS was established to grab the growth opportunity that would come from the oversold US stock market. Now the US market is at all time high and the company returned outstanding returns.
